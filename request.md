## Description
Standard linear regulator both for postive and negative fixed voltages.

## Specifications
* Voltage: 3.3V 5V 9V 12V -3.3V -5V -9V -12V
* Ampere: max 500mA
* Outputs: 1
* Dropout: n/a
* Quiescent current: n/a
* PSSR: n/a
* Package: any SMD
* Form factor: smallest form factor
* CASTELLATED PCB
